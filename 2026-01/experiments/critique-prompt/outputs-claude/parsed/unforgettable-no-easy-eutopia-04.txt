The authors use the sailing-to-an-island metaphor to set up three factors: island size, navigation, and multiple attempts. They announce they'll address only factor (1), the island's size. If this confusion is corrected, the paper has much less to say about target size than it claims, and the sequel essay on convergence must bear far more argumentative weight.
